1) Quick Summary

CamShield helps you identify devices on your Wi‑Fi network, discover nearby Bluetooth devices, and use camera-based color filters intended to help reveal infrared light sources.

Important: The App is designed so that scan results and device lists are handled on your device. We do not intend to collect the list of devices you scan unless you explicitly share it with us (for example, by sending screenshots or logs to support).
  • We collect limited information needed to operate the App, deliver subscriptions, prevent fraud, and improve performance.
  • We may use analytics to understand how the App is used and fix issues.
  • We may use advertising identifiers only where allowed and, on iOS, only when you grant permission (App Tracking Transparency).
  • You can opt out of tracking permissions and control what you share.

3) Permissions the App May Request

The App may request access to certain device permissions to provide core functionality. You can change permissions at any time in your device settings.

Camera

Used to show a live preview and apply visual filters intended to help reveal infrared light sources.

Bluetooth

Used to discover and list nearby Bluetooth devices where supported by your OS.

Location (may be required by OS)

Some platforms may require location permission to perform Bluetooth scanning. We do not use location to track your precise position unless you grant it and your OS requires it for scanning.

Tracking (iOS App Tracking Transparency)

If presented, you can allow or deny tracking. If denied, we will not access the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for tracking purposes.
